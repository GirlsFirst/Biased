Religious bias

Religious bias is treating a person or group differently because of the particular beliefs which they hold about a religion. Religious bias can happen anywhere, but it is commonly seen in the work place. Religious discrimination occurs if an employer makes an employment decision based on your religion, creed, or sincere belief, rather than your job performance or qualifications. For example, if you receive excellent job evaluations until your supervisor finds out you are Muslim, and then you are terminated, this is religious discrimination.

What is the cause of Religious Bias?

Religious bias comes from lack of understanting, ignorance, and the mindset that your own religion is right so everyone else's is wrong.
