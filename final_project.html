Are You Biased?
By: Angelina, Robyn, and Kailynn

Our mission

Our mission is to bring awareness to bias in our society. we want people to know where their bias falls. We are all biased; whether intentionally, or unconsciously. We want to show how one's bias affects their decisions, interactions, and even self-image.

General Definition

Bias is showing an unreasonable like or dislike against an idea or thing, usually in a way that is closed-minded, prejudicial, or unfair. Biases can be innate or learned. People may develop biases for or against an individual, a group, or a belief.
